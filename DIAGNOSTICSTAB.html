DIAGNOSTICS

Diagnosis is a skill that can only become acquired by many years of practice. The technicians of today do not all have the same diagnostic skills that this job requires. The older technicians that spent years honing those skills are all gone. If you take training classes or research the information on your own in order to obtain these skills, it could take a lot longer for a technician to get up to speed on these skills. So what do we do about it? The goal of this area on Toyota Top Repair is to take you through the process it takes to come up with a diagnosis, but more importantly to hand you the information and proven skillsets that help to complete a diagnosis faster. If it does not make sense to you by text the hope is that other things such as pictures and videos will. The idea is that by seeing how it is done and then actually doing it, you will learn quicker as well as retain the information that you are given here. And thats the key. Retention. A link to what tools you need will be provided in the future once the information becomes available.
