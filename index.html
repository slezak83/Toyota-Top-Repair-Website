  Quick Tech Tip: The tacoma has been the source of numerous issues as we all know. The 2017 tacoma has now been found to have good spots underneath the lower intake manifold for mice and rats. If you get a code for an open circuit on one or more of the direct injection injectors (p020213 cylinder 2 for example) I strongly advise you to make sure there is a real open circuit or short, remove the upper intake and look for a mouse or rat that has chewed through the sub harness very near the D4S connectors under the throttlebody.
